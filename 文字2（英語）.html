Aozora Student, 2nd Year

Title [Day Trip]

August 10th (Saturday) Early in the morning, we went to Tenkawa Village in Nara Prefecture with our family. It was a very hot day, and we were excited to go swimming in the river even before we went. As soon as we arrived, my father and mother prepared a barbecue, and my sister and I went to swim in the river. There were a lot of fish swimming in the river, and the water was very cold, so we forgot about the heat and swam a lot, and even jumped in from the water. For lunch, we had meat and grilled salted amago trout. It was really fun playing in the river and having a barbecue in the middle of nature. On the way back, we stopped at a hot spring called "Mizuha no Yu" and took a bath in the open-air bath while looking at the mountain scenery. It was a day trip for the family, and on the way back in the car, we all said, "It was fun. Let's go again."

That's all.
